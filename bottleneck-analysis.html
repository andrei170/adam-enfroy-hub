Three things are missing inside that stack. None of them are about the product. The product is built. The audience is built. The masterclass is built. The Skool community is converting. What is missing is the funnel infrastructure that connects the assets to the lane above the Skool tier.

The three things, ranked by revenue impact

01. No short bridge VSL on the Skool sales surface

The masterclass funnel at incomeblueprint.ai/training pre-sells the Skool community at 747 USD. That works for cold traffic that opts in and watches 80-90 minutes of training before joining.

It does not work for the warm YouTube subscriber who clicked through from a flagship video like "How I Make 50k a Month with AI in 2026" and lands on a Skool-native "JOIN GROUP" button with no warm-up. The 193K YouTube channel is already pre-selling the buyer; the Skool surface is the conversion endpoint that does not catch them.

Fix: A 5-9 minute Adam-fronted bridge VSL on a dedicated landing page with the Skool checkout below. Routes YouTube end-card clicks, Instagram bio-link clicks, podcast-appearance referrals, and any non-masterclass-funnel cold traffic into the Skool community at a meaningfully higher rate.

Install window: Days 1-3.

Year-1 floor math: A bridge VSL on the Skool sales surface conservatively lifts conversion 1.5x to 2x on non-masterclass traffic. At your Skool join volume, that is mid-five-figures to low-six-figures incremental Year-1 from the Skool tier alone, before any of the application-tier revenue gets counted.

Cost of waiting: Every month the bridge VSL is not live, the warm YouTube and IG traffic continues to land on the bare Skool surface. The 193K YouTube subscriber base compounds; the conversion rate on those subscribers stays at the bare-Skool rate.

02. No application or scorecard above the 747 USD Skool tier

The Skool community is the public ceiling. Weekly live calls with you, 8 phases, 37 lessons, 15 AI frameworks at 747 USD. The "JOIN GROUP" button is the only public CTA on the Skool sales surface.

There is no lane above that for the operator who has already passed the 10k-a-month outcome the Skool tier promises. No application form. No scorecard. No 1-1 inquiry route. No done-with-you implementation tier. The student who reached the Skool outcome and wants the next stage (programmatic SEO at scale, AI workflow industrialisation, paid traffic, productised-consultancy build) has nowhere to go inside the AdamEnfroy.com ecosystem.

This is the highest-leverage structural gap. The historical BGE alumni (thousands of paid students across 2020-2024) plus the current Skool community of several thousand alumni plus the 193K YouTube audience all converge on a 747 USD ceiling.

Fix: A 12-question "AI Blog Income Stage Scorecard" that segments respondents by current monthly revenue. The ten-k-plus branch routes to an application form for a high-ticket done-with-you implementation tier. You and Colin (with the existing AdamEnfroy.com team) deliver the application tier; AG1 builds the scorecard, the application form, the routing logic, the Calendly wire-up, and the landing-page surface.

Install window: Days 4-7.

Year-1 floor math: Tightly-capped, application-gated, high-ticket. Even at a conservative cap of 4-8 application accepts per month at a five-figure price point per engagement, that is six-figures Year-1 on the application tier alone. Result varies with where you and Colin cap the application accepts and what fulfilment model you scope for the tier.

Cost of waiting: The historical BGE alumni and the Skool graduates with no upgrade path keep cycling through partner-affiliate offers, competing courses, or no purchase at all. The application lane is the lane that captures them.

03. No nurture sequence routing masterclass attendees and BGE legacy alumni into the application tier

The masterclass funnel nurtures into the Skool tier at 747 USD. That is the only destination it pre-sells.

Once the application tier exists, the masterclass attendee list and the historical BGE-alumni list need a second route. Some attendees self-identify on opt-in as "already past 10k a month" or "want done-with-you, not done-yourself". Some BGE alumni from 2020-2024 hit the 10k-a-month outcome and never came back to a higher-tier lane because no higher-tier lane existed. The nurture sequence is what gets them off the Skool-only path and onto the application path.

Fix: Two parallel email sequences. Sequence A: post-masterclass attendees who self-segment as "already past ten k" or "want done-with-you" route into a 5-touch application-pre-sell sequence ending with the scorecard, then the application form. Sequence B: a BGE-legacy-alumni migration sequence (5-touch) routes the historical BGE buyer list into the same application lane, framed as the next-stage tier built specifically for students who already finished BGE / AI Income Blueprint.

Install window: Days 5-7 (after the scorecard and application tier surfaces from Bottleneck 02 are live).

Year-1 floor math: The nurture is the connective tissue, not the destination. The math attaches to the application tier (Bottleneck 02). The nurture lifts the application-tier intake from the existing list assets by 2-3x versus an untargeted application lane. Tied to Bottleneck 02 math.

Cost of waiting: The application tier opens cold to new traffic only. The existing list assets (masterclass attendees, BGE alumni, Skool community) are the warmest possible application-tier audience and they sit untouched until the nurture is wired.
