What AG1 actually builds for this prospect (service-to-asset mapping)

AG1's core service catalogue: - VSL call funnels (primary) - Webinar funnels (primary) - Email sequences and nurture flows - Landing pages - Tech setup and integrations (Stripe, Skool, Typeform, ConvertKit, Zapier, GHL) - Sales infrastructure (application form + setter / closer routing) - Future-service: paid ads (Meta, YouTube)

Mapped against Adam's existing offer stack:

  1. Short bridge VSL + dedicated landing page sitting between cold YouTube / IG traffic and the Skool checkout. The Skool "JOIN GROUP" surface is Skool-native and minimal. A 5-9 minute Adam-fronted bridge VSL on a dedicated landing page with the Skool checkout below converts cold YouTube subscribers and IG viewers at a meaningfully higher rate than routing them straight to the Skool surface. AG1 builds the VSL script, the landing page, the Skool checkout wire-up. Adam and Colin keep the Skool community exactly as it is scoped today.

  2. Application-gated done-with-you tier above the 747 USD Skool community. A 12-question "AI Blog Income Stage Scorecard" that segments respondents by their current monthly revenue (zero, low-three-figures, low-four-figures, ten-k-plus). The ten-k-plus branch routes to an application form for a high-ticket done-with-you implementation tier where Adam and Colin (with the existing AdamEnfroy.com operations team) ship the programmatic SEO + AI workflow + paid traffic build for the advanced blogger. AG1 builds the scorecard, the application form, the routing logic, the Calendly wire-up, and the sales-page surface for the application tier. The tier itself is delivered by Adam, Colin, and the existing team.

  3. Nurture sequence from masterclass attendees into the application tier + BGE-legacy-alumni migration sequence into the same lane. The masterclass attendee list currently nurtures into the Skool tier at 747 USD. A second nurture branch routes the attendees who self-identify as "already past ten k a month" or "want done-with-you" into the application tier. A separate BGE-legacy migration sequence routes the historical BGE buyers (thousands of paid alumni) into the same application lane. AG1 builds the segmentation logic, the email sequences, the routing infrastructure, and the cross-sell timing. The application lane stays the destination; the nurture is the connective tissue.
